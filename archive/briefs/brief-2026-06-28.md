Your vault already half-knew this: media-buying's own slop-warning reads *"Volume amplifies a winner, doesn't create one."* Nick just named what the winner has to BE — an angle AI can't flatten.

Quietly significant: it's the **first capture in weeks about keeping a buyer** rather than buying one. Every recent brief has been acquisition (ads, pages, quizzes, review domains); [[sms-flows]] is the back-end finally showing up.
