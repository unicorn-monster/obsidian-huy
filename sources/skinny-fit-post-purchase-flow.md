Structure documented:
1. **Checkout order bump** — additional supplements offered aggressively at the initial checkout step.

**Congruency note:** the sequence is logically staged, not random — first two OTOs are supplements meant to accelerate the result already promised, the next two introduce a complementary product (tea + stirrer) framed as boosting fat-burning further, keeping each upsell thematically connected to the last.
