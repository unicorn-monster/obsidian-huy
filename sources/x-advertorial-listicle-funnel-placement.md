## HookRate funnel-placement taxonomy (verbatim)
Source: @hookrate_ (verified), Mar 18 2026, 28.9K views.
> if you're an ecomm brand and you're not testing:
> ad > pdp
> ad > advertorial > pdp
> **ad > advertorial > pdp > rtg listicle > pdp**
> ad > sales page
> ad > low ticket FE > upsell
> ad > quiz funnel > personalised offer page
> ad > vsl > offer page
> **ad > listicle > pdp**
> rtg ad > product demo vsl > pdp
> …every month…

**Key takeaway — listicle has TWO valid slots:**
1. **Cold front-end presell**: `ad > listicle > pdp` (listicle warms the prospect before the PDP).
2. **Warm RETARGETING layer**: `rtg listicle` — re-engage people who already saw advertorial→PDP and bounced. = external confirmation that listicle/comparison is a **warm-traffic** format (matches [[presell-pages]] format table: listicle cold→warm, comparison warm).

## DTC Midas landing-page taxonomy (verbatim)
Source: @DTCMidas thread.
> A landing page is simply any page someone lands on after clicking an ad. That could be: product page · home page · collection page · **advertorial · listicle · long-form pre-sell page · VSL page**. In this case, I was mostly referring to the last four.

→ Listicle = one of his 4 core **presell** page types (advertorial / listicle / long-form pre-sell / VSL). Also (separate post): *"Creative isn't just the video/static — it's video/image + primary text + headline + landing page + identity page"* → landing page + identity page are part of the creative unit Andromeda rewards for diversity (ties to [[maxpro-ugly-ads-plan]] Andromeda / persona-network note).
