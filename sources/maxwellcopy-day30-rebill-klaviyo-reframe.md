The HIGHEST CHURN day for almost any subscription brand is day 30.

That's the day someone gets charged for the first time on their rebill.

And the email that triggers the cancellations from users is just the default Shopify confirmation email.

Right when the customer sees this, their stomach drops, they get scared, and a lot of them cancel asap.

THE FIX:

If you're a Shopify Plus brand, you can turn that scary transactional Shopify order confirmation off and rebuild it in Klaviyo with different framing.

Still tell people they just placed another order.

Just reaffirm the product benefits, remind them what they get with their subscription, and make it look like it actually came from your brand.

They still got charged, but are way less likely to rage-cancel over it now.

And it looks more like a brand worth staying subscribed to.
