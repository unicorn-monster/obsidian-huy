THE BLACK FRIDAY GOOGLE ADS ADVANTAGE — a last-minute guide to print on BFCM with a new marketing channel. The exact 5-day (really 6-week) preparation system.

## WHY MOST BRANDS FAIL BFCM

Panic phase (2 weeks before: scramble, random promos, feed not optimized) → Act phase (rush ad copy, GMC promotions take 3-5 days to approve, tracking broken) → Black Friday (campaigns barely approved, promos not showing, budget too low, no warm-up data, competitors dominate auction, make 20% of potential). Prepared brands make 3-5x more than unprepared — not better products, better SYSTEMS.

## STEP 1 — BASELINE FROM LAST YEAR'S DATA (6 days before, 2-3h)

Shopify Analytics (Oct-Nov): AOV, conversion rate, daily revenue — find when the spike began, strongest week, peak-day revenue, sustain-or-drop pattern. Example: normal AOV $85 → BFCM AOV $120, spike began Nov 22 (3 days before BF), BF > Cyber Monday, CVR 2.1%→4.8%.

Google Ads BFCM week: spend/ROAS/CVR by day, which campaign types won. Example: normal spend $500/day → BFCM $2,500/day; Shopping ROAS 8.2x, PMax 6.1x, Search 11.3x (highest), wasted $800 on Display (1.4x) → pause Display during BFCM.

**Competitor Auction Insights (secret weapon most ignore):** Google Ads → Shopping campaign → Auction Insights → compare Oct vs Nov impression share by competitor. Reveals who scales hard during BFCM (threat), who backs off (opportunity), new entrants. Action: raise bids 30-50% during BFCM if your impression share dropped.

## STEP 2 — EARLY WARM-UP CAMPAIGNS (4 weeks before, 3-4h)

"Preheat the oven" — warm the audience before BFCM so they're ready. Early-access landing page (countdown + email opt-in, tease without revealing offer) + Demand Gen campaign ($10-20/day, YouTube/Gmail/Discover, "Our Biggest Sale Is Coming"). Expected: 10-30k impressions, 200-500 clicks, 20-50 signups. Storytelling arc across 4 weeks: mysterious tease → hint at discount → reveal offer w/ early access → countdown reminder. Warm traffic converts 3-5x better than cold; people need 5-7 touchpoints before buying.

## STEP 3 — OPTIMIZE SEARCH CAMPAIGNS (3 weeks before, 2-3h)

**Scheduled headlines (secret weapon):** Ads & assets → Headlines → create BFCM-specific headlines ("50% Off Black Friday Sale") → schedule start date → PIN to position 2 (position 1 = main value prop, position 3 = secondary benefit). Result: CTR +40-60%, Quality Score improves.

**Promotion extensions:** Extensions → Promotion → select occasion "Black Friday" → discount type/amount → start/end dates → promo code. Shows badge inline in the ad. CTR +30-50%.

**Sitelink extensions:** BFCM-specific sitelinks ("Black Friday Deals", "Best Sellers 50% Off", "Cyber Monday Bundles", "Free Shipping Over $50") scheduled to the BFCM window — more entry points, more screen space.

**Price extensions:** show 3-8 products with strikethrough/sale price directly in the ad. Require MANUAL update at midnight before BF (can't be scheduled) — set a reminder.

Combined effect: ad takes up 3-4x more screen space, pushes competitors down, CTR +50-100%, Quality Score improves, you pay less per click and rank higher — compounding advantage.

## STEP 4 — OPTIMIZE PRODUCT FEED (3 weeks before, 3-5h)

Shopping drives 50-70% of BFCM revenue. Title formula: `[Main Keyword] - [Key Feature] - Black Friday Special - [Discount] - [Brand]` — must include "Black Friday"/"Cyber Monday" or you don't show for those searches. Description: first 150 chars lead with the offer + specific problem solved + authority + urgency + deadline. **Sale-price sync is critical:** Shopify sale price must exactly match GMC sale price or products get disapproved — verify Shopify update → GMC check → live-ad check on a strict timeline. BFCM images: existing product shot + "50% OFF"/"BLACK FRIDAY" badge overlay, text <20% of image (Google guideline), 1200x1200 min, test badge vs clean version.

## STEP 5 — GMC PROMOTIONS (2 weeks before, 1-2h)

Submit 2 weeks early — approval takes 3-5 days, sometimes longer; submitting 3 days before BFCM means missing it entirely. Setup: Merchant Center → Promotions → unique promotion ID → destination "Shopping ads" → applicable products → offer type (%/$/free-ship/BOGO) → promo code → submit. **Must test at checkout before submitting** — Google actually tests your checkout flow; if the promo doesn't apply correctly there, it gets disapproved. Common mistakes: submitting too late, promo broken at checkout, wrong timezone/dates, promo code mismatch between GMC and actual code. Impact: CTR +40-70%.

## STEP 6 — SEASONALITY ADJUSTMENTS (2 weeks before, 15-30min) ⭐ advanced/underused

Tools & Settings → Bid Strategies → Advanced controls → Seasonality adjustments. You tell Google "expect +50% conversion rate during this window" so it bids aggressively from the start instead of learning too late (by the time normal smart-bidding "learns" BFCM behavior, BFCM is over) — then auto-reverts after. Rules: only use ≤14 days (longer confuses the algorithm; BFCM's 3-4 days is ideal), be conservative on the estimate (+40% not +100% — overestimating kills profitability by overbidding), apply to Shopping/Search/PMax only (not Display/YouTube — they don't scale well with it), don't touch budgets manually during the event once set.

## STEP 7 — FINAL REVIEW (1 week before, 2-3h)

Per-campaign checklist (budget multiplier, scheduled headlines, promo extension live, feed/assets updated) for Branded Search / Shopping / PMax / Exact Match. Budget math from last year: normal day spend $500→revenue $2k (4x) vs last BFCM $2,500→$15k (6x) → this year target $3,000/day→$18k/day (6x), 4-day total ~$12k spend/$72k revenue/$35k+ profit. Backend checklist: sale prices live, promo codes work, checkout tested, mobile fast (<3s), sufficient inventory + backup suppliers, conversion tracking verified end-to-end, support staffed. Do one full real test-purchase through the actual ad before go-live.

## STEP 8 — EXECUTION (BFCM week, 1-2h/day)

Nov 28 12AM: activate price extensions manually, verify seasonality adjustment live, confirm GMC promos showing, test-search to confirm visibility. Then monitor 3-4x/day (8AM/1PM/6PM/11PM) tracking spend pacing, ROAS, impression share (<70%→raise bids), conversion rate (should be 1.5-2x normal). **Don't panic** — conversion lag is 24-48h, what looks bad in real-time often reads great by Saturday. Don't make big changes (no major bid moves, no pausing) — monitor, don't meddle. Don't compare hour-to-hour, only day-to-day. Cyber Monday = usually 60-80% of BF volume, more desktop, often higher AOV — don't drop guard.

## STEP 9 — POST-BFCM RETARGETING (1 week after, 2-3h) ⭐ the 10-15% most people leave on the table

Buyer psychology: BF = overwhelmed/decision paralysis/"I'll come back"; 1 week later = FOMO kicking in, less overwhelmed, ready to decide. Build 3 audiences (site-visitors-no-purchase, add-to-cart-no-purchase, initiated-checkout-no-purchase) via Audience Manager, excluding converters, from the BFCM date window. Campaign: Demand Gen/Display, $30-50/day, 7-10 days. Messaging must NOT claim "Black Friday extended" (people know it's fake) — instead smaller, realistic discount with new urgency ("Missed Black Friday? 25% off until Dec 5", "Last Chance: 30% off ends this week"). Expected: $300-500 spend over 10 days, 30-60 conversions, 8-15x ROAS (very high, warm traffic), $3-8k revenue = ~10-15% additional on top of BFCM total.

## STEP 10 — DEACTIVATE & REVIEW (after BFCM, 1-2h)

Turn off all BFCM-specific extensions/headlines/promotions/seasonality adjustment, revert titles/descriptions/images/budgets to normal. Pull year-over-year comparison table (spend/revenue/ROAS/conversions/AOV). Document a "BFCM retrospective": what worked, what didn't (e.g. Display underperforms, inventory gaps, mobile checkout bugs), what to change next year (start prep 8 weeks not 6, 2x inventory, fix mobile checkout pre-Q4, skip Display, +30% budget).

## THE FULL 6-WEEK TIMELINE (checklist form)

5 days before: pull last year's data, auction insights, plan offers, set targets. 4 days before: early-access LP live, Demand Gen warm-up launched, email list building. 3 days before: feed titles/descriptions/images updated, scheduled headlines + extensions configured. 2 days before: GMC promotions submitted, seasonality adjustment set, full test, inventory/payment checked. 1 day before: final structure review, confirm approvals, test full purchase flow, support prepped. BFCM week: activate price extensions manually, monitor 3-4x/day, don't panic, don't touch budgets. After: launch retargeting, deactivate BFCM elements, review, document learnings.

## TOP 5 MISTAKES

1. Waiting until November — GMC promos won't approve in time, no warm-up data, miss 40-60% of potential revenue; start 6 weeks minimum. 2. Not testing checkout — Google actually tests your promo code path; untested = disapproved. 3. Panicking on Black Friday itself — conversion lag means real numbers show up 24-72h later; don't cut budgets on same-day "bad" ROAS. 4. Forgetting post-BFCM retargeting — leaves $5-15k on the table. 5. Not documenting learnings — repeats the same mistakes next year.

## THE OPPORTUNITY MATH

Baseline: $100k/mo revenue, $30k/mo profit. With proper BFCM prep: Nov revenue $100k normal + $70k BFCM boost + $10k post-BFCM retargeting = $180k, at better blended ROAS (6x vs 4x) → ~$70k profit in November alone — roughly 70% of a normal quarter's profit in one month.
