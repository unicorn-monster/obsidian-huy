## Quiz Funnel

Positioning of the quiz is essentially the most important part of the quiz

You want a promised outcome

an advertorial or listicle needs an open loop : which subconsciously promises a close loop

A quiz needs a promise too

In this case:

**Find your over thinking score and stop it for all**

The promise here is so clear and strong

It sets up exactly WHO and WHAT they’ll get from watching this

![Image](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEv3VfYbIAA56jR?format=jpg&name=large)

This funnel is structured VERY similarly to Livens

In fact, almost all elements are identical

**P.S:** If you want to clone the liven quiz you can use the template from Clarflow in a 1:1 clone

Here is the quiz template: [https://some-moles-like.clarflow.com/self-discovery-assessment](https://some-moles-like.clarflow.com/self-discovery-assessment)

30 questions. Zero mention the product.

5-stage psych funnel:

**Self-label → Pain deepen → Cost expand → Hope reframe → Future cast**

Each stage makes the next one inevitable.

**Stage 1 — The Self-Label Trap (Q1–7)**

Gender. Age. "How often do you overthink?"

Zero friction. Just answering about yourself.

But by Q7 you've said "always" and "as long as I can remember."

You didn't answer a quiz. You told yourself you have a problem.

Commitment chain — started.

![Image](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEv3djsaQAAIVE7?format=png&name=large)

**Stage 2 — The Agreement Barrage (Q8–17)**

"I find myself stuck on the same thoughts." — Agree scale.

"I imagine every possible scenario that could go wrong." — Agree scale.

10 straight questions where the only honest answer is "yes, that's me."

Every "Strongly Agree" = deeper investment.

By Q17 you don't THINK you overthink.

You KNOW. And it's ruining your life.

![Image](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEv3g-wXAAA1k1O?format=jpg&name=large)

**Stage 3 — The Cost Expansion (Q18–19)**

Now it asks how overthinking affects your CAREER.

Miss opportunities. Feel judged. Not good enough.

Pain in your head is ignorable.

Pain in your wallet is not.

![Image](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEv3mUSa0AAbS-A?format=jpg&name=large)

**Stage 4 — The Hope Injection**

Right after the career damage — an interstitial:

"87% of overthinkers are more empathetic and intelligent."

18 questions making you feel broken.

Then — your flaw is actually a SUPERPOWER.

It just needs the right tool.

Perfectly timed.

![Image](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEv3p2vbYAAcWxL?format=png&name=large)

**Stage 5 — The Pseudo-Diagnosis**

Overthinking score: 32.33 — RED. Overthinking type: "Worrier." Duration. Impact areas. Self-doubt level.

Clinical-style visuals. Alarming colors.

This isn't a quiz result.

It's a diagnosis. And the prescription is loading.

![Image](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEv3uAhX0AApVpb?format=jpg&name=large)

**The pattern:**

Hook → Label → Deepen → Expand → Reframe → Diagnose → Capture

Never sells directly.

Makes you co-create a "personalized plan" for 3+ minutes — so claiming it via email feels like picking up something that already belongs to you.

![Image](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEv3xYtWAAAfnm9?format=png&name=large)

What to steal:

— A strong reason and promise immediately

— Open with zero-friction demo questions (age, gender) before ANY emotional content

— Use agree-scale statements to force self-diagnosis — not questions, STATEMENTS they confirm

— Place social proof interstitials BETWEEN question clusters, not before or after the quiz

— Expand pain from feelings → tangible cost (career, money, time) before offering hope

— Make the result page look clinical — scores, types, color-coded severity

The quiz IS the conversion mechanism.
