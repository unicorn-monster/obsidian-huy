Most brands have thousands of people on their list who signed up, got the welcome offer, and never bought.

An easy win you can send this week is a plain text email from the founder.

Just them talking like a real person explaining why they started the brand, three quick reasons customers love it, and store credit to get them over the line.

Since it doesn't look like marketing, it gets opened and read completely differently than a designed campaign.

Here's the exact template you can copy:

Sender Name: [Founder First Name] | [Brand Name]

Subject Line: "Hey, quick thought..."

"Hi there,

It's [Founder First Name], the founder of [Brand Name].

I wanted to personally reach out and say thanks for checking out [Brand Name].

A lot of people come across us because they're looking for [specific problem your product solves], and I totally get it. That's why I started [Brand Name] in the first place. [Brief 1-2 sentence personal story about the brand's origin or mission.]

If you're on the fence, no pressure. I just want to share a little about why customers love us:

- [Unique Selling Point 1]
- [Unique Selling Point 2]
- [Unique Selling Point 3]

And, I just activated store credit in your account for $XX. Just use code [Code] at checkout in the next 24 hours.

When you're ready, here's a link to check out our bestsellers:

[Link to Shop Bestsellers]

Thanks for being here. It means a lot.

[Founder First Name]

Founder, [Brand Name]

P.S. If you have any questions or want a recommendation, just hit reply. I actually read these myself :)"

Works as a campaign, in welcome flows, abandoned cart, winback, etc.
