title: Creatine survey reveals LTV unlock via habit

The biggest LTV unlocks come from literally ASKING YOUR CUSTOMERS instead of guessing what they want…

There was a creatine brand that was bleeding subscribers because people weren't taking their creatine every day… so since they weren't seeing results or just had too much product piled up, lots of people were cancelling.

So they ran a dead simple survey to his customers:

> What results are you seeing?
> How long did it take and how happy are you?
> How do you actually take the product every day?

They then plugged those answers into the customer data and found one thing that changed everything…

They found that the customers who took their creatine with their morning coffee had over 50%+ higher LTV than everyone else!

Makes complete sense when you think about it...

You already drink coffee every day, so the creatine goes down with it… You take it daily, you consume it every day, you see results, and you've got no reason to cancel!

So the entire front-end message became one line: "Put creatine in your coffee."

All from just one survey and plugging the data into Claude.

It's always the unsexy stuff…
