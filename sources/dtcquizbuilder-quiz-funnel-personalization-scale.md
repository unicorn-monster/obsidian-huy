The way we find out info has changed too

Books > Google > AI

The way consumers buy has changed too

DTC > Amazon > TikTok shop

Mass content, tech & info means we want everything tailored to us. Solve OUR problem

Which is why ads need to be niched down to win in the auction

It's why headlines need to be specific

It's also why quiz funnels are brilliant sales processes

You can personalize an offer at massive scale

Think about a sales call, a good salesman doesn't just yap about why their car tires are the best

They ask you questions, speak to you, it feels conversational

You just yap away the entire time

That by the end of it he knows what triggers you.

You told him you hate coming back every 4 months
You love off roading but the tires mess up
What sort of car you have

Now he says:

"Oh these are great because you won't need to come back every 4 months and are perfect for off roading"

If he just told you what to buy. In the back of your head you're thinking - "This brother doesn't even know what I want tires for"

We've become spoiled with options & that's why we want to believe that this option is the best for US
