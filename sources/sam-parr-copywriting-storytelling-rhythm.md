Interview Sam Parr tren podcast Sweat Equity "Become a Dangerously Good Writer in 55 Minutes". Doan 17:13-27:40: co che slippery slope + nhip cau + storytelling. (Co 1 doan quang cao Motion ~19:01-19:53, khong phai noi dung.)

## Slippery slope = Joe Sugarman
Axiom trong sach Joe Sugarman. Cau kinh dien: "muc dich cua cau dau la khien ban doc cau thu hai, muc dich cau thu hai la khien ban doc cau thu ba" va cu the. Khong can 100% mua, chi can dung nguoi doc het ad -> mot phan lon se mua.

## Rule of consistency (don tam ly dang sau)
Neu da lam 1 viec nho cho ban, nguoi ta co xu huong lam tiep viec lon hon. Study kinh dien: xin dan sticker nho o cua so truoc, 2 tuan sau quay lai xin cam bien to ngoai san -> ty le dong y cao hon ~40%. Copy cung vay: khien nguoi ta bat dau doc = ho se doc tiep (bat dau truot doc).

## Rhythm / nhip cau
Viet nhu nhac: cau ngan, cau vua, cau dai, cau ngan. Dung ramble cung mot do dai cau. Cac rule cho nguoi moi:
- Viet o trinh do lop 7. Check bang hemingwayapp.com. USA Today = lop 4, New York Times = lop 7.
- Mot cau = mot y. Neu dinh dung dau phay, thuong nen thay bang dau cham.
- Bat dau cau ke bang "And" / "But" de tao nhip (transition words).
- Warren Buffett: giai thich thu cuc phuc tap (bao hiem/Geico) bang cau don gian. Do dai cau trung binh trong thu thuong nien tut tu 25 chu (khi tre) xuong 17 chu. Bi quyet = constraint -> clarity.
- Hemingway (The Old Man and the Sea): cam xuc sau nhung noi cuc don gian.

## Killing your darlings / write with your eraser
Viet draft xong cat 1/3, roi cat 1/3 nua, roi cat tiep. "Write drunk, edit sober." Ogilvy: "Toi la writer te nhung editor gioi." Cat buoc ban noi dung thu can noi, khong lan man clause.

## Story, story, story
Khong co "qua dai", chi co "qua chan". Cach lam interesting nhat = bien thanh story (mo dau - than - ket + chut tension).
- Wall Street Journal "Tale of Two Boys" chay 28 nam, hon $2 ty doanh thu. Sam an cap nguyen xi cho publication Trends ($300/nam) cua The Hustle, "twist 3%", va no crush.
- Hero's journey / StoryBrand: khach hang la HERO, brand la GUIDE (WSJ la nguoi dan cau be thanh cong). Dung de brand lam hero.
- Founder story Sam cho Hampton: khoanh khac ban The Hustle, ngoi voi 14 lawyer/accountant, thay overwhelmed -> "uoc gi co nhom peers" -> ly do lap Hampton. Story + hero's journey + "but/therefore" (kieu Trey Parker/South Park: moi beat noi bang "but" hoac "therefore", khong phai "and then").

## Failed solution (absolve guilt)
Thay vi nhoi benefit vao mat, go toi cho nguoi xem: "Ban da thu giai quyet van de nay bang X nhung khong duoc. Do khong phai loi cua ban, chi la ban thieu dung nguyen lieu." Bac cau sang phan sau.
