title: Bootstrapped brands win on creative not budget

'Those who can afford to spend the most to acquire a customer win'

Yes that is true, but if you are bootstrapped you don't really have that luxury (especially in the beginning)

Unfortunately you need to be so much better at creatives and landing pages than the big boys if you want to have a shot at competing

Most supplement brands at scale would simply not think these numbers are possible

Yet people get triggered when I say that majority of the category leaders ads are objectively mid (Gruns, IM8 etc)

Of course we are shooting for higher CPA's now but this is what it took to scale so fast
