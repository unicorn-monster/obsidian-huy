title: Billing reminder AB test reduced cancels 33%

If you are a subscription brand, you MUST be AB testing your billing reminder emails.

One of the biggest drivers of churn is a scary email that says "you're about to be charged, you can cancel here"

We decreased cancels for this brand by 33% from this AB test.
