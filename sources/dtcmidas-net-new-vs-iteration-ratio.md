title: Ad volume — net new vs iteration ratio
---

For volume, launch as much ads as you can without diminishing quality.

I'd rather launch fewer but high quality (not talking about production quality) and high conviction ads.

The net new vs iterations ratio shouldn't be static.

You test new things to figure out what works.

Whenever you find something that works, you iterate the shit out of it until it stops working.

You'll have periods where you're 20% iteration, 80% net new. And periods where it's 50/50.

It depends on where you are in the cycle.
