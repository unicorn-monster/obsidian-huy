## YouTube Ads
Their YouTube ads are significantly better.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q5FqwKx048M

![[0-400-000-site-visitors-in-60-days-08.png]]

What they're doing that's very interesting is the weird pages that lead you to a secret website. With no instructions, just a password. The book hides a "real quest" and you get real rewards if you get it. This is their USP. While people are searching for more physical interactive things to do together, this is a great way to do this. Totally opposite end of the spectrum from our last breakdown (AI Arbitrage method).

Their book does something interesting which is use a real angle that has problem / solution & fear built into it — surviving an apocalypse. But their real selling point is the mystery. The book is mysterious (the visuals in the ads), the secret website and the password. It's something that becomes fun to do, builds engagement and has real physical prizes built into it.
