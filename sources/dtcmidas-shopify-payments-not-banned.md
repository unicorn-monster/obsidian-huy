title: How to not get banned from Shopify payments
---

It's not that hard to not get banned from Shopify payments.

Just ship the product, don't promise what you can't deliver on, offer (good) customer service, and if you run subs make sure customers can cancel them without going trough some crazy flow.

Honestly makes your life easier (and more fun) not having to deal with payment processing issues and beeing able to focus on growth.
