You're leaving money on the table by only running ads from your brand page. 50% of our ad spend goes through 3rd party pages. It works well because it looks less like an ad. Here are a few examples: - Authority pages: dermatologists, doctors, athletes, trainers, nutritionists - Creator pages: partner with creators for whitelisting, or create "real person" pages that look like your target avatar. - Niche community pages: "Moms of Toddlers", "Plant-Based Living", "Home Gym Warriors" - Magazine/blog posts pages: "The Health Guide", "Beauty Review", "Wellness Insider"
