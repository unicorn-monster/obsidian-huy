title: Awards certifications as third-party trust signals

Here's how brands become the obvious #1 choice in super aware markets.

All your competitors claim the best quality, the best reviews, the best formula, etc...

So customers have no way to tell who's actually telling the truth.

The easy way to stand out are awards and certifications.

A third party badge is a great way to back up your claims and earn more trust.

Most of these awards are not too hard to obtain if you put in the effort

For some you just need X amount of votes (easy if you have a big email list), others you can just buy.

Here are a few examples:

- Beauty: dermatest, vogue beauty awards, allure best of beauty

- Health: nsf certified, men's health awards

- Tech & gadgets: red dot, ces innovation award

On top of that every country has local consumer awards. "voted product of the year" for example runs in 40 countries.

Use them in your ads, lp's, pdp's and see your performance improve!
