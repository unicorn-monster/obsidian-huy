Scaling native ads to 3k/day comes down to this:

Show the exact pain your customer has, in a super detailed way.

Here are 5 nano banana image prompts that can help you:

---

Back Pain Prompt:
Candid photo of a man in his mid-50s stepping out of a silver four-door sedan in a grocery store parking lot on an overcast afternoon. He is half-standing with his left leg out of the car and right leg still inside, one hand gripping the top of the car door frame for support, the other hand pressing firmly into the small of his lower back with fingers digging in. His face is turned slightly toward the camera showing a tight grimace, jaw clenched. He is wearing khaki cargo shorts, a faded navy polo shirt, and white walking sneakers. A reusable grocery bag is visible on the passenger seat behind him. Flat overcast daylight, no harsh shadows. Shot from about 10 feet away in the parking lot as if another shopper walking by took the photo on their phone. A metal shopping cart is partially visible at the right edge of the frame. Slightly grainy, smartphone camera quality, not composed or staged, natural muted color tones.

---

Restless-Leg-Syndrome Prompt:
Overhead photo of a woman in her late 40s lying in bed at night, white cotton sheets half kicked off and bunched around her calves. One leg is bent at an uncomfortable angle with the knee up, the other stretched out straight with toes pointed tightly. Her right hand is gripping her left calf muscle, knuckles slightly white from pressure. Her face is turned toward the nightstand, eyes open and visibly exhausted, mouth slightly parted. She is wearing an oversized faded sleep t-shirt. A small bedside lamp on the wooden nightstand is on, casting warm yellow light across the bed and her face. The rest of the bedroom is dark. A digital alarm clock on the nightstand displays 2:47 AM in red numbers. A glass of water sits next to the clock, half empty. The sheets are wrinkled and messy, the pillow dented and pushed to one side. Shot from above at a slight angle, as if someone standing at the foot of the bed took the photo. Low light, visible grain, smartphone quality, warm yellow color cast from the lamp.

Neck Stiffness Prompt:
Candid photo of a man in his early 40s sitting at a cluttered home office desk in a small room. He has both hands clasped behind his neck with elbows out wide, eyes squeezed shut, mouth slightly open showing visible discomfort. A silver laptop is open in front of him showing a dense spreadsheet with rows of small numbers. An empty white coffee mug sits to the right of the laptop with a brown ring stain inside. His phone is face-down beside the mug. He is wearing a wrinkled light blue button-down shirt with sleeves rolled unevenly to the elbows, no tie. The desk has scattered papers, two pens, a tangled phone charger cable, and a small sticky note pad. Behind him is a plain off-white wall with a single small window showing grey daylight. Overhead ceiling light mixed with the cool blue glow from the laptop screen illuminating his face from below. Shot from the doorway of the room, about 8 feet away, slightly off-center, as if someone walking past the door paused and took the photo. Unposed, natural, smartphone camera quality with slight softness in focus.

Joint Pain Prompt:
Candid photo of a woman in her late 60s standing in a small kitchen, holding a glass jar of red pasta sauce in her left hand at chest height and gripping the metal lid tightly with her right hand, visibly straining to twist it open. Her knuckles are slightly red and her finger joints appear mildly swollen. Her face shows a mix of frustration and physical effort, lips pressed together, brow furrowed, looking down at the jar. She is wearing a simple light green short-sleeved housedress. The kitchen is modest with older white appliances, beige laminate countertops, a wooden knife block near the stove, and a striped dish towel draped over the oven door handle. Through a window above the sink, warm afternoon light falls across the counter and her arms. A cutting board with an uncut yellow onion and a box of dried spaghetti sit on the counter behind her. Shot from across a small kitchen island, about 5 feet away, as if someone else in the room took the photo while she was struggling. Foreground counter edge slightly visible in frame. Sharp focus on her hands and face, background slightly soft. Smartphone camera quality, warm natural tones, no filters, no editing.
