title: SMS welcome flow adds $109K on top of email

So many brands are running a welcome flow with just email and have zero SMS touchpoints in it.

Why would you leave that on the table?

If you take a look at this client's welcome flow, they did $887K from email alone this year.

But their SMS welcome series brought in an EXTRA $109K on top of that.

Without SMS that $109K just may not exist. Those are customers who didn't open the email or needed that extra nudge on a different channel to convert.

And a text message is what got them over the line.

It's one of the simplest and easiest lifts you can make to your core flows.
