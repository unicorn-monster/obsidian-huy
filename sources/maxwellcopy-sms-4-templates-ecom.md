I've sent texts to tens of millions of people on behalf of my $1M+/mo ecom brands.

These are the 4 SMS that consistently outperform everything else (steal them):

SMS #4: The testimonial.

A real customer quote will always land harder in someone's texts than it does sitting in an email.

It shows up right next to messages from their actual friends and family, so it reads more like a recommendation than an ad. That context does a lot of the convincing for you.
