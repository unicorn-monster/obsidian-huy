Unwritten rule of email: Your headline angle > your copy

People don't really read your copy.

But you can change just the headline angle, touch nothing else in the email, and still double or triple the revenue off it.

Take a dog food brand:

A weak angle would be something like "help your dog live longer."

Sure it's true… But nobody's actually going to buy with intent from that.

A banger angle would be "you want your dog to live forever. So feed them like it."

That pokes you in the chest a little and it's the stuff that will cause buying behavior.

Here's how to find a banger angle:

1/ Start with the boring benefit. "Helps with sleep."

2/ Find the emotional pain behind it; "Waking up at 3am every night and feeling exhausted"

3/ Find the tension. "Wrong supplementation may be the reason you wake up at 3am every night"

4/ Add some special sauce: "The source of your exhaustion lies in your medicine cabinet"

Once you've got your banger angle, the rest of the email doesn't matter.
