My unpopular opinion is that AI has officially hit a plateau.

We're at a point now where we're actively DECREASING our AI usage.

We've basically moved off AI for copy because it's just bad at it lol…

Our best copywriters end up spending more time editing the AI than if they'd just written it from scratch.

The second somebody senses AI in your copy… alarm bells go off and you lose all chance of converting.

It comes across as non-genuine and that you are lazy marketer…

But on the flip side, our AI usage for tools and systems has gone way up…

We built our own analytics tool with AI and it's been a huge differentiator.

Not to mention we're also building full project management systems with it and other stuff we didn't even know could exist before AI.

AI has always been great at some things and bad at others.

Less AI copy… more AI systems.
