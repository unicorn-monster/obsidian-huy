## Web2App method

Web Apps are notorious for running cracked quiz funnels, with insanely long, direct response flows & mechanisms

A famous one is MadMuscles we've spoken about extensively

Another 2 killer examples:

Pawchamp & Liven

So what makes the Webapp email capture method unique?

1. **Minimise confusion on offer page**
2. **MORE DATA**
3. **0 drop off**

Let me explain

**Minimise confusion on offer page**

Pop-ups can add discounts on top of other discounts

Get people off the PDP (when they're ready to buy) to an email

Confuse them with an offer on top of your offer

Or spazz out and show up as they click an ad

Spikes CPA on meta & kills conversion

Ton of room for fuck-ups

Most of you aren't super multi sku or launch focused either, one product with a super short conversion cycle. No need for pop up & can minimise the risk of something going wrong

**More Data**

Imagine not only exporting the users email into klavioy, then throwing them into a generic flow or email campaign.

You actually export their email with every answer they made as a variable

{{age}}

{{main problem}}

{{Desire}}

{{length of problem}}

{{failed solutions}}

You get the point

This allows emails to be SOOO much more valuable and hyper targeted.

Flows on specific problems, campaigns on specific desires, etc..

This is also why I say everyone should have their email agency testing [@clarflowcom](https://x.com/@clarflowcom) just for sending this data back to klaviyo

**0 drop off**

The hardest & most challenging. It requires split tests & tweaks to nail.

Web apps do this incredibly well

Look at something like Pawchamp

![Image](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HKHcr2XXIAAYdh_?format=jpg&name=large)

They're thinking about the email capture already from their positioning

You're getting a free, personalized K9 training program

By the time you get to the email capture...

You've already been sold so hard on the main product & why this well help you, that the additional "value prop" , is just an extra step and you're getting something by giving your email. It's simply a 3rd party to receive your good - not a big ask

![Image](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HKHdFDhXMAADzJx?format=jpg&name=large)

This shift of turning the email into an intermediary to recieve something 'k9 discipline challenge as opposed to feeling like the customer is giving you valuable data, is how they capture emails, with minimal drop off

Still get the sale on the front end

Then use all your answers to upsell you more products down the line

While knowing your biggest problems, goals and desires
