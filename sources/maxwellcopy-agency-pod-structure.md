1 year later and we have the same setup lol

Just more pods and more divisions.

A few slight changes (with goal of giving more depth):

By raising our prices we made the margins work where pods only manage 4-6 clients instead of 7-8 so clients get more strategy, more time, and better service.

Each division owner (Senior AMs) only manage 3 pods now instead of 4. We found that's the best balance for the level of service and touch points we want to provide while making margins make sense.

We also added a Director of Service to keep leveling up our service quality and hold the standard, alongside our Director of Ops who are both co-overseeing the seniors.

This structure is amazing and sets you up to scale.

Don't plan on changing it, besides continuing to raise prices to where pods manage even fewer clients, as little as 3-4, to get even higher touch points.
