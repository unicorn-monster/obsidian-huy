3 non-discount emails you should add to your calendar:

1. The Viral Video + Comments
We all know that short-form video gets people to convert. If you have a video that pops off, create an email about it, and be sure to include the comments of people advocating for you.

2. Deep Founder Newsletter
If you have a forward-facing founder, the long-form newsletter-style campaigns crush. People wanna buy from people, not corporations.

3. Elephant In The Room
Most brands have one juicy, hairy objection that almost every customer has. Address that straight up and label it as the Elephant In The Room. It builds trust because you're speaking directly towards what the customer is concerned about.

Some of our top performers in recent months.
