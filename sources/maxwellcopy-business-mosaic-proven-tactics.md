title: Business as mosaic of proven tactics not copying brands

You're literally going to get fucked if you just copy other brands shit.

The reason these big brands / companies grew to the level they're at was NOT by following somebody else's playbook completely…

By the time some brand blows up and you try to copy their model, they've already got the first mover advantage, and their economics are so good at that point that they can just outspend you and keep growing while you're sitting there having a hard time scaling trying to be them.

You see a lot of brands try to follow these 9-figure brand models when they literally sell flowers. That's not going to work lol.

But it is incredibly important to watch what other fast growing companies are doing and take little pieces here and there…

Your business should be a mosaic of a ton of other proven tactics, mixed with your own judgment and whatever's unique about you.

That's what eventually turns into something completely its own.
