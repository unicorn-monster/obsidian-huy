This VSL ad has been running since Dec 2024, and has generated over 40,000,000 impressions...

40,000 likes and over 4,000 comments.

This is the perfect example of a top-of-funnel, cold audience VSL ad creative.

Here’s why it prints money 👇

1\. This is a typical "educational" VSL.

It's designed to build massive authority and desire by teaching the viewer a "secret process" that only the product can deliver easily.

It reframes the problem of hair loss from an irreversible tragedy to a scientific process.

Its entire structure is built to create a series of "aha!" moments that logically lead the viewer to conclude that this specific product is the only complete solution.

This is one of the few ways to unlock cold audiences effectively.

2\. A hook that creates hope

Think about the impact of hope on someone who’s losing his hair and think it’s a permanent loss.

This creates instant buy-in and makes the viewer desperate to learn the "secret".

3\. Unique Mechanism

Every good VSL ad presents a unique mechanism that is different to what people have tried in the past and aims to re-position the product into something that they have never tried in the past.

4\. 3D visual elements

These elements make the invisible visible. This serves as proof for the scientific claims being made and help the viewer understand the mechanism.

5\. Selling against the competition

An essential part of every VSL.

It’s a sophisticated way to handle objections prior to purchasing. It positions the product as more superior and more complete to anything else the viewer has tried in the past

After the whole educational part where the whole selling is done, the product is introduced as the only viable solution.

There is literally nothing more powerful than a good direct-response ad that creates hope.
