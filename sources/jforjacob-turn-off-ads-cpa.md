I genuinely think the whole 'never turn off an ad' thing is the dumbest thing in ecom

Peopled have been so psy-oped by this

Can't tell you how many times we've had ads taking up spend in a CBO that are above target, we turn them off, CPA goes down massively, MER improves and we scale spend up as a result

Of course this is obvious to anyone outside the DTC twitter bubble but it's amazing how many people seem scared to do this
