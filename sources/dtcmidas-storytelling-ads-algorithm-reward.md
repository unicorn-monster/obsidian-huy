You won't unlock new scale with your ads until you become better at storytelling. Humans have always used stories to pass down knowledge, teach lessons, and shift beliefs. The ancient Greeks couldn't explain thunder and lightning. So they told a story… Zeus, king of the gods, throws lightning bolts down from the sky when he’s angry. Suddenly a terrifying and unexplainable event was understandable. That's what stories do, they make sense of the world in a way facts alone can't, it's how our brains are wired. And that same wiring is why storytelling works so well in ads today. When people scroll, their guard is up. They know what a normal ad looks like and they skip it instantly (in this scenario you already paid for that view and got nothing back) But a story told in a format that's native to the platform bypasses their guard and lets you put a narrative in their head. That's why a story can completely shift how someone thinks about their problem and how to solve it. Instead of you telling them your product is the best for xyz (which they'll resist), the story leads them to that conclusion themselves. There's a chemical reason why this works too. A good story triggers dopamine (which drives attention and memory) and oxytocin (which builds trust). Storytelling ads also help you reach more new people. Because good stories hold attention and drive engagement, the algorithm sees them as content people actually want to consume. So it pushes them into colder audiences and rewards you with lower CPMs. Compare that to a straight hard-selling ad that only reaches the small pool of people already in-market. Good stories get you into the bigger, colder audiences. And those people enter your funnel already believing in your solution because they've consumed your story. The formats working best for us right now: - AI Animation Ads (these come in so many fun formats, once you have your messaging dialed in, go ham and test a lot of variations) - Yapper Ads - Native statics + long form copy So here's what to do next: put your phone in your pocket, go for a walk without any music, podcast, or scrolling tiktok, just raw dog it, and while walking try to think of ideas for stories around your current best working angle. Then write down your ideas in your notes app. Then come home, make yourself a bit less sober using your drug of choice, normally a beer and a ciggy does it for me, and try to write a good storytelling ad and run it. Do that a few times and get better at it. That will be your next unlock.
