## Spoken transcript (captions, verbatim)

Today we're going over the top e-commerce subject lines and preview texts.

First off, for any cart or checkout abandoned email, **"Your order is ready to ship."** You can look at this AB test, but we consistently see this outperform anything like "forgetting something" or "you leave your cart behind."

Next, we have **the empty preview text.** Just have no preview text like this. Super simple — in Klaviyo's new text-based editor you just check this box.

Three, the cold emails are going to love this one. **"Quick question, {first name}."** It just always rips in getting opens. Of course you want your headline to be some sort of question, but it just absolutely crushes.

Next, we have the intrigue factor. **"Didn't plan on this."** Great for something like new product drops or a secret sale.

Next, always **using some sort of number or list.** People just love it. So "3 reasons why XYZ" or "3 reasons XYZ."

Then we got the scare factor. **"Beware."** You can literally have it just be this, or you can have it be a full sentence.

Next, just **end your preview text or even a subject line with "..."** (dot dot dot). I tested this — it always gets more opens and it builds a lot of intrigue.

For any sort of offer, if you're giving something away, you can do the **"We owe you XYZ."** Like if you do a BOGO or something, say "Hey, we owe you a t-shirt." Somebody sees a subject line like "we owe you," then you're like "Oh, I got to open this."

Next, we got **"POV:"** — works really well for the younger audience.

Then you have **"3. 2. 1..."** It's great for any sale. Just having numbers stands out in the inbox.

And then you have **"Unlocked: [gift]."** Then it seems like it's personal for the person, like "I unlocked this." Steal these right here.
