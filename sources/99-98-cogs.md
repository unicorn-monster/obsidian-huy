@KrsteKovaceski shared this tweet which shows an AI offer (creating songs for people) absolutely ripping. He shares some of the economics below. And this is absolutely GENIUS.

![[99-98-cogs-01.png]]

(Tweet, @KrsteKovaceski: "Hats off to whoever runs PrayerSong 🎩 Cleanest AI arbitrage I've seen: Engine: Suno (<$1 COGS) · FE: $50 · Upsells: Rush delivery ($50) + more · AOV is likely $80+ with huge margins. They aren't selling 'AI music'. They're printing selling tears and memories." Meta Ad Library screenshot: PrayerSong, ~1,600 results, launched December 2025.)

They're hedging on 'most people will never use AI for this' which is totally true. They have taken the simplest AI action (API calls) which could be built without any coding tools. They framed it as: Our Band will create this song for you. It gets even more genius on 7 day delivery time — makes it perceived as real, creates pay-to-speed-up-the-process opportunity.

But the real genius here is in how niche they have gone. It's not just 'songs for people.' It's 'Christian Music for someone you love.' They identify their niche: Christian Music & Christians. Emotional Angle: Selling tears, love & connection. Some angles that CRUSH for them:

![[99-98-cogs-02.png]]

Where it gets even crazier (for us) is how easy this is to do. These questions are fed straight by an API call into kie.ai (which has SUNO's API). All they have done is: created the front end with a no-code builder; created a 7-day pause to email this customer (probably a data-set in case an email is incorrect → they can find it and re-send).

![[99-98-cogs-03.png]]

So what are the opportunities here? Will let's find whichever AI models are popping, you can get creative with. Immediately I think: take this into other niches → songs for grandparents. Change language (my favorite) — ripping this thing exactly the same but Spanish would print so hard. Other AI (old photos to videos) or (kids pictures to coloring-in books). Find good AI models & get creative with it. These are incredibly simple to make too.

Their Ads: we already looked at how simple their landing page and funnel is. But their ads are even MORE simple. Which is the genius behind a product with a slight bit of complexity — most ecom guys won't try to figure out any AI stuff.
