title: Odd email send times beat flat hours inbox competition
---

Easily one of the simplest tests we've started doing that I can't believe we weren't doing before.

Every brand sends their emails at flat hours. 9am. 10am. 12pm…

The problem is your customer opens their inbox at 9:01 and there's literally 5 emails from 5 different brands all sitting there from the same exact minute…

Yours gets buried instantly.

So we started sending at random odd times nobody else is hitting. Like at 1:09am. 4:03pm. 5:08pm…

The result is your email shows up ALONE in someone's inbox instead of competing with 4 other brands for attention. It just sits there by itself waiting to get opened.

We pulled this up in @hiroanalytics and you can actually see every send time across all of our campaigns, sort by day, revenue, open rate, and whatever you want.

The odd send times are consistently outperforming the flat hours.

Go try it on your next send.
