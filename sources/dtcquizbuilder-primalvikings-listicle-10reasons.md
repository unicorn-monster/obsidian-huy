## Landing Page Techniques & Take-aways

**The Comparison Table Up Top**

Before they even start reading, there's a side-by-side nutritional comparison. Reindeer vs beef.

This anchors the entire page.

You don't have to convince them organs are good — they already believe that.

You just have to convince them they picked the wrong one.

(much cheaper to convince existing organ believers reindeer is better, than non believers to try reindeer organs)

**The Listicle Format (10 Reasons)**

Each reason follows the same pattern: here's something you thought beef organs did well → reindeer does it better, and here's why.

It's competitive destruction disguised as education.

![Image](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HBrG3SpX0AAFlrI?format=jpg&name=large)

A few examples of how each reason moves them:

- "Built for the Wild" → naturalness, purity angle (beef = farm raised, reindeer = untamed)
- "Raises Testosterone Through the Roof" → hits the core desire for the 30-55 male demo
- "Speeds Up Weight Loss and Makes Man Boobs Disappear" → pain point agitation with a specific visual image

![Image](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HBrHHPNWwAATerW?format=jpg&name=large)

- "Thrives in -40°F Where Cattle Wouldn't Survive" → positions reindeer as genetically superior. Borrowed authority from nature itself
- "Very Low in Toxins" → makes you question what's in the beef organs you're currently taking
- "Results in Days, Not Months" → speed of result, overcomes the "I've tried this before" objection

**The Awareness Progression**

The page walks the reader through a very clean belief shift:

1. **Problem-aware** → You're already taking organs, good. But you're not getting the full benefit.
2. **Mechanism-aware** → The issue isn't organs. It's the source. Beef organs are inferior across every metric.
3. **Solution-aware** → Reindeer organs fix every gap beef organs leave behind.
4. **Product-aware** → This specific product (Primal Viking) is the way to get reindeer organs.

They never had to sell organs as a concept.

They just redirected existing belief toward a new mechanism.

**Urgency & Close**

Valentine's Day sale with countdown timer, 60% off, "Last Chance" framing.

Classic scarcity close after the belief has been fully seeded.

By this point the reader isn't deciding if they should try reindeer organs — they're deciding if they can afford to keep taking beef ones.
