title: Cross-sell myth: repeat buyers want same product
---

"Cross-sell everything!"

This is the most misunderstood and overrated advice I see in ecom…

Instead, the data often point towards selling MORE OF THE SAME.

Let's say someone buys a t-shirt…

Most brands will do everything in their power to push them to bags, then pants, then whatever else they sell.

But the data says to do the exact opposite!

We pulled the repeat purchase data on this 9-figure brand in WellTV (our own retention analytics platform), and over half of repeat orders are the same product again, just in a different style.

Someone who buys a t-shirt is literally TELLING you they're a t-shirt buyer.

Quit with the cross-sells… make your primary repeat purchase message around more of the same.

Same applies to supplements… if someone buys creatine don't jump to cross-sell to protein.

Consider up-selling to other flavors and boosting LTV that way.
