I tracked 11 DTC supplement brands on SimilarWeb.

9 of them are running native ads.

Finding 4: Session duration

Landing pages that had majority native traffic, had a longer session duration than "meta only" landing pages.

Learning: the native audience is much more attentive and WANTS to find out more about your product.
