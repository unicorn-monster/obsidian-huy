There’s an old copywriting rule you’ve probably heard 9,000 times:

“Enter the conversation already happening in your prospect’s mind.”

Sounds smart.

Sounds GENIUS even. 

But…………

It’s actually surface-level as fuck.

Because most of your prospects are not walking around having some clean, conscious conversation in their head.

Think about it.

If 95% of all the thoughts we have on a daily basis are subconscious, how in the f*ck are we as advertisers going to enter that measly little 5%.

Most thoughts are subconscious, we all know this.

It’s not like our customers are thinking:

“I am dissatisfied with my current identity and would like to purchase a product that resolves this tension.”

That’s not how people work lol.

Most of what drives a purchase is happening beneath the conscious layer of language.

And after spending 10s of millions of dollars on ads I’ve deduced that there are really three different layers of DEPTH that you can speak to your prospect at.

Level 1: The mind.

This is the standard advice.

Find the words your prospect is already thinking…

Then mirror those words back to them.

Boom.

Simple.

And to be clear…

This still works.

Yes, customer language matters.

But it only catches the part of the decision your prospect can consciously explain.

Which is usually not the part actually controlling the decision.

Level 2: The body.

People buy on emotion and justify with logic.

We all know this.

So instead of targeting the words in their head…

You target the emotional state in their body.

Fear.

Shame.

Desire.

Hope.

Relief.

Now we’re getting closer.

Because emotion moves people before logic ever gets a vote.

But I still don’t think this goes deep enough.

Level 3: The psyche.

This is where David Hawkins’ scale of consciousness gets very interesting for marketers.

His framework says that people occupy different levels of consciousness at different moments.

Shame.

Guilt.

Apathy.

Fear.

Desire.

Anger.

Pride.

Courage.

And upward from there.

But consciousness is NOT the same thing as emotion.

Emotion is the physical experience.

Brain chemistry.

Sensation.

The shit happening in your body.

Consciousness is the deeper lens through which the person is experiencing themselves and the world.

It’s metaphysical.

It transcends physical reality itself. 

And THAT is the level that 99.99999% of marketers will never even touch.

Two prospects can feel the exact same emotion…

But be calibrated at two completely different levels of consciousness.

Which means the same message can pull one person toward the sale…

And push the other person away from it.

Ya see…

The real job of a marketer isn’t just to know what the prospect is thinking.

Or even what they’re feeling.

It’s to know them so fucking well that you can pinpoint where their consciousness is calibrated right now.

Then build the message from there.

Bc that’s the deepest you could ever possibly speak to your customer. 

Because once you understand the level they’re occupying…

You understand what they can hear.

What they’ll reject.

What they’re reaching for.

And what kind of message can actually move them.

That’s a much deeper game than “use the words from the Amazon reviews ” lol.

This concept is really hard to explain just in an email…
