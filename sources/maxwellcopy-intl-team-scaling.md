title: International team 3 reasons to scale agencies

Building a mostly international team is the biggest unlock I've had scaling Well Copy.

I have 90 people on my team and only 10% are based in the US.

Here are the 3 reasons why:

1. The talent pool is genuinely better and deeper internationally. Almost every large email marketing agency hires this way, so we're able to headhunt people with a ton of real agency experience who are really good at what they do.

2. It's cheaper… which means we can charge lower prices than we could with a fully US based team, and that makes our clients happy.

3. In general, they work harder. A lot of them really want to work for a US company, so if you pay them well they're thankful for it and they bring energy every day. US people I've found are a bit arrogant and lazy at a higher rate.

So what do we actually use US based people for?

Copy and sales.

We're mostly serving US brands, so a US based writer just gives you better, more relevant copy.

And for sales we want the people who are front facing to match the customer.
