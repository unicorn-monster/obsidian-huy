Interview Sam Parr tren podcast Sweat Equity "Become a Dangerously Good Writer in 55 Minutes". Doan 33:11-40:30: Sam viet lai 3 ad do ngay tai cho. Doan swipe-worthy nhat.

## Nguyen tac chung
Ad do thuong nem thang san pham vao mat ("daily restorative drink") ma chua build desire, chua co ly do de tin.

## Case 1 - AG1 "AGZ" sleep supplement
Ad goc: "the nightly drink for restorative sleep". Sam dua 2 huong:
- Proof / transformation: chi 1 anh whoop/sleep-score before-after, KHONG show san pham. "93% nguoi gio ngu 8 tieng."
- Educational story theo AIDA (Sam bia het so lieu de minh hoa framework):
  - Attention: "Co ly do khoa hoc vi sao sau tuoi 35, 75% dan ong tinh giac luc 2-3h sang."
  - Interest: mo ta dung cam giac ho dang trai (tinh giac khong ro ly do) -> "suot 5 nam ban tuong day la do tuoi tac, la binh thuong."
  - Desire + "not your fault": "Su that la do ban thieu vitamin D... khong phai loi cua ban." Roi "tin tot la de fix" -> moi dan ra san pham.
  - Social proof: "Trusted by 100,000 dads just like you."

## Trojan horse
Chon san pham that sau trong story, build desire lau roi moi lo. Trai nguoc ad do nem san pham ngay giay dau.

## Yes-ladder (get "yes" som)
Dat cau ma doi tuong chac chan gat: "Chang phai kho chiu khi tu nhien toi tuoi 35 ban khong ngu noi qua 3 tieng?" -> ho gat -> "Biet khong, day la hien tuong moi, khong phai luon the." Dua may cau nay len SOM NHAT co the trong copy.

## Case 2 - Caraway non-toxic food storage
Ad goc: "store more and stress less". Sam bao viet tu dau thi dung Claude lay stats, roi:
- Hook dam mat: "Ban co biet trong hop Tupperware nhua 1 nam tuoi co nhieu E.coli hon ca bon cau?"
- Headline hinh anh manh hon: "Imagine storing your food in the toilet bowl. Because that's exactly what you're doing." -> giai thich -> "that's why glass is better."

## Case 3 - Kids clothing (free game)
"Ban co bo sua con vao coc nhua roi quay lo vi song toi khi coc chay ra khong? Tat nhien khong. Nhung do chinh xac la dieu ban lam khi mua pyjama polyester roi bo vao may say." -> organic cotton.

## Nguyen ly xuyen suot: reframe bang hinh anh cuc truc quan + so sanh gay soc
- Con so truu tuong vo nghia. "Bap rang rap co 150g chat beo" = vo cam. Nhung do day 1 coc mo / cam 1 tang mo "day la luong mo trong 1 bich bap" -> am anh.
- "Deconstructed Coke": tach ra "40% cho nay la duong."
- Luon frame vao mot thu NO ro rang (ai cung noi khong voi "cat do an trong bon cau").

## Meta-note Sam noi ve chinh minh
"Toi khong gioi copywriting, chi la da so nguoi ta do. Toi nhu thang biet choi vai bai guitar o tiec, the la duoc goi la nhac cong gioi."
