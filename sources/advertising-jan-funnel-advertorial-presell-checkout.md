This Is Why You Are Poor.

Your ecom funnel looks like this:
Ad → PDP → Cart → Checkout

Basically you're saying:
"We send traffic to our PDP and then they can figure out the rest"

And you wonder why you struggle to go above 2k adspend per day...

When did "letting customers figure it out by themselves" ever work?

This is what a real ecom funnel should look like (save this)
Ad → Advertorial → Presell → Checkout

You see what's happening?
You stop asking the customer to figure out everything on their own.

You tell them EXACTLY the next step, and make it as easy as possible to convert.

Customer thoughts on PDP:
- "ohh let me see the bestsellers"
- "what other categories does this brand have"
- "Let me read their founding story"
- "Oh they were published on vogue? Let me read the article"

You see? WAY TOO MANY distractions.

Your funnel has one goal: conversion

Delete everything that doesn't increase the likelihood of that...
