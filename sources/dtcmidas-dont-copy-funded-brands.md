title: Stop copying funded brand funnels — wrong unit economics
---

Stop taking inspiration from these big brands.

Well, most of you shouldn't.

Almost all of these brands are funded and have completely different unit economics than you, which means they can sustain way higher CACs and won't run out of cash.

On top of that, they've been running a while and/or have celebrity endorsements, which means they've built up a level of trust and brand awareness.

When you copy their funnels and ads, you're copying a strategy built on advantages you don't have.

The best brands to take inspiration from are the ones that are bootstrapped and scaling quickly.
