Interview Sam Parr (founder The Hustle, co-host My First Million) tren podcast Sweat Equity: "Become a Dangerously Good Writer in 55 Minutes". Doan 0:00-17:13 = phan copywriting fundamentals, chu de "the slippery slope". Gan het kien thuc Sam thua nhan la stolen tu 2 cuon sach copywriting co dien (Ogilvy + 1 cuon it noi hon) va pickup-artist books.

## Slippery slope (Sugarman)
Demo ngay o intro: "You're reading this not because you want to, but because I want you to. Now you're reading this second sentence because again I'm forcing you to." Moi cau chi lam 1 viec: keo nguoi doc xuong cau tiep theo. Do la truot doc tron.

## Copywriting = tam ly, khong phai chu
Hai dinh nghia cua Sam:
- Copywriting khong phai viet chu. La hieu nguoi ta muon gi va lam sao khien ho lam dieu minh muon, roi moi chon chu.
- "Getting what's in my head into yours and influencing your behavior."

Muc tieu KHONG phai 100% nguoi mua. La dung nguoi doc het ad, va mot phan lon trong so do se mua. "No one will read this, it's too long" = sai; dung ra la "no khong du interesting".

## AIDA
Attention - Interest - Desire - Action. Sam ke dung AIDA de cua vo bang 1 joke lentil/hummus o happy hour (grab attention truoc). Nguyen tac flirting = sales: noi gi cung duoc mien lam ho cuoi va ro rang la khong harmful. "Restaurant owner energy" = noi chuyen nhu da quen tu truoc, dung stiff.

## Headline = 80% ngan sach
David Ogilvy: trung binh gap 5 lan nguoi doc headline so voi body. Viet xong headline = tieu 80 cent trong 1 dollar. Ogilvy ton ca tuan chi de viet 1 dong: "At 60 mph the loudest noise in this new Rolls-Royce comes from the electric clock." Xu huong moi: gioi tre (YouTube) biet hook/packaging quan trong hon ca noi dung, nen viet hook truoc chu khong con viet article roi moi de headline.

## Mind the gap / curiosity gap / open loop
Tao cang thang buoc nao phai doc tiep de giai toa (giong nhac phai duoc resolve). Con nguoi khong chiu noi cliffhanger. Vi du kinh dien Hopkins: "They laughed when I sat down at the piano, but then I started to play" (ban khoa hoc nhac). Cung nguyen ly: Upworthy/BuzzFeed "top 10 reasons... you won't believe number 7". Short-form ngay nay phai STACK open loop chong open loop, chen curiosity gap moi ~8 giay.

## Long converts, ugly converts (mien la interesting)
Sam phan bac "attention span ngan di": ban chat con nguoi khong doi, luon phai lam cung 1 viec. Dai gan nhu luon convert tot hon. Dan chung: VSL 27 phut chay truoc YouTube video (cuoc rang khong ai bam skip), funnel 9 con so cua dan affiliate GLP-1 (telehealth). copythat.com: 2000 chu chu den nen trang, convert ~5%.

## Time constraint (tu pickup-artist books)
Dat rang buoc thoi gian de nguoi ta chiu nghe: "tao phai di trong 1 phut nhung hoi nhanh cai nay". Dua vao copy: "cai sap noi se blow your mind, nhung nhanh thoi de tao ke...". Snap nguoi ta vao. Tony Robbins lam gioi vu nay trong sach 800 trang: "chuong nay tao ke X, nhung doi den chuong sau moi la thu blow your mind" (giong intro YouTube script).

## Niche targeting trong headline
"Niches make riches." BuzzFeed "50 reasons you're from Denver Colorado" (chi hop nguoi Denver nhung 30% bam vao). Homesick Candles = nen mui tung bang (nguoi o bang do mua, nguoi da roi bang mua tang me). Hampton ad: "neu ban kiem hon $3M/nam thi toi biet chinh xac ban cam thay the nao" roi noi cuc than mat voi nhom hep. Series theo cap do wealth (1M / 10M / 100M) duoc Inc + Forbes dua lai.

## Twist cau quen thuoc
Lay phrase noi tieng be lai cho brand. Porsche "You may get lost, but not in the crowd". VW/Porsche joke "pick up 9x more women than a Lamborghini". Churchill "we shall fight on the beaches" va "the only thing you have to fear is fear itself" = khuon cu phap (specific + curiosity gap + payoff cuoi). Co sach chuyen cho ~10 kieu phrase co the steal (Dharmesh HubSpot recommend). Alliteration + syntax co matter.

## First sentence phai dam
Cau dau phai punch. Case study The Hustle ad: "My friends think I'm smart. I'm not. I just read The Hustle" kem mat Ben Franklin (ban dau la mat Mark Cuban toi muc bi gui cease-and-desist). Sam noi ad do "singularly responsible for the exit", tieu ~$10M chay no, convert ca chinh nguoi phong van.
