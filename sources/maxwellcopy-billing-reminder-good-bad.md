Bad Billing Reminder vs Good Billing Reminder

Bad:
- Opens with what you're about to be charged with
- No reminder of why they subscribed
- Same email for every product and every order number

Good:
- Gets them excited about the order and frames it as part of their routine
- Reinforces the perks of being a subscriber
- Skip and delay buttons so they push it back instead of canceling
