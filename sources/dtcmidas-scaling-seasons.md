title: Scaling seasons framework bootstrapped 7-fig

We hit multiple 7 figures / month bootstrapped by knowing when NOT to scale.

Ecom growth is not linear.

There are seasons where scaling feels easy.

Everything you launch works, and budgets and revenue go up daily.

And you have seasons where every ad you launch flops and efficiency tanks.

That's normal, and it will happen over and over again.

It's important to scale hard when the market and algorithms allows for it and it goes easy.

Don't try to force scale when it's not scaling season, in those periods it's better to bring down your spend to protect your margins.

That way you can deploy more cash into scaling season.

Each scaling season should increase your baseline, that way you'll grow long term.

But don't use slow season as cope, keep focusing on your inputs (eg. testing creatives, offers, launching products, etc.) to get back to scaling - eventually you will.
