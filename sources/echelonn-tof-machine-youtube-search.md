### 2\. The Role of TOF in a Scalable Google Ads Funnel

TOF can mean different things to different people.

But when we talk about Top-of-Funnel, we’re referring to campaigns that target people who haven’t heard of your brand, aren’t searching for it, and might not even know your product exists.

These people are cold, but they’re not random.

They’re in your ideal customers with a problem your products can solve. And with the right message, they’re absolutely capable of converting right now.

This is the biggest segment of your market.

If we had to put a number on it, we’d say more than 90% of your ideal customers actually live in the top of funnel.

Which means…if you can unlock this segment, there’s almost no cap on how far you can scale.

We set up one of these campaigns (that I’m about to show you) for a brand, and it brought in over $406,000 in new revenue from traffic they had been ignoring for years.

This dual effect—driving sales and lifting the entire funnel—is what makes TOF campaigns so powerful.

#### B. TOF Search Ads

These campaigns focus on people who are actively researching a problem your product solves.

“How to lose belly fat”

“Best protein for beginners”

“Natural ways to reduce bloating”

This is where intent is high and competition is often low.

![](https://rhetorical-garage-a1e.notion.site/image/attachment%3A4379abff-efc6-4422-bff3-78ef24f9e5d0%3Aimage.png?table=block&id=239a60cf-8ed8-8104-96c8-e3d15da0b10f&spaceId=d9fb0601-20b1-4779-a1c6-d68592cdd197&width=1330&userId=&cache=v2&imgBuildSrc=requestProxiedImageUrl)

For every commercial keyword you're targeting, there are typically 5-10 related informational keywords with much higher volume and lower competition.

People searching these terms are ready to learn and open to trying something new.

If your ad shows up with the right message, you can turn that curiosity into conversions.

We once set up TOF Search campaigns like this for a client and started getting high-quality clicks for as little as $0.07.
