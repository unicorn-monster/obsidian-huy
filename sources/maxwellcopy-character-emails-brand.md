title: Character emails humanize brand inbox presence

Character emails are crushing right now.

People want to buy from people, so instead of sending every single email from your brand name you send them from actual humans on your team.

A few reasons why they work so well:
- They humanize the brand. People buy from humans they like.
- They're a pattern interrupt. A new sender name stands out in an inbox.
- They unlock messaging you can't do in brand voice. Can do stories, heads ups, and cheeky stuff like that.

Who it can come from:
- Alex from the warehouse, giving an almost sold out heads up.
- Megan from customer success, sending a head up email
- Alice from marketing, hijacking the account for a cheeky discount.

Nobody's stopping you from doing stuff like this lol

Pick a few people, give them a name and a personality, and let the emails come from them.

Easy, fun, and it works.
