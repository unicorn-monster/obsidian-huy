How to fix your quiz start rate

17% start rate on the first funnel.

83% land and bounce before a single question.

Second funnel — 31%. Nearly double.

Same traffic. Same category.

One difference - continuity from the ads

The start rate lives in the continuity

Two things have to line up:

1. What the ad is saying
2. What the first question is saying

The ad breaks off mid-promise. Q1 has to meet customers where they're at, promise them a reason to keep going and a way to continue

Think about your avatar scrolling instagram on her couch

Ad hits a nerve. Big claim. Curiosity spiked.

Then Q1 opens with a generic, unaligned question

They're back at square 1 - there's not continuity

So how do you build continuity?

Ad makes a promise. Q1 pays it forward.

The positioning meets them where they're at and gives them the next steps

Feels like the next step, not a form.

They started because the ad kept its word.

An easy way to fix this is to put them side by side:

What is the ad saying?
What is the first question saying?

If Q1 doesn't match the underlying problem with a way to reach their desire...rewrite it

Continuously A/B test this.
