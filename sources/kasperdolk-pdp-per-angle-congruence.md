Chad knows it better than anyone in the market that personalization and matching your creative with an LP/funnel is the no.1 way to scale

If you're new to LPs/listicles, you can even just duplicate the product page and make it specific for that angle

We had a client selling orthopedic slippers a few years back (when everyone was just running straight to PDP).

When I opened the back-end I saw 30 products. Exact same image + setup.

I thought client went mad and just tested different pricing and offers

But once I opened the PDPs,

I just saw he had 30 PDP templates with:

\- different product title per avatar/angle

\- different imagery in product slider

\- reviews by ICP instead of general

\- all copy written for that ICP/angle

1 was for diabetic patients, 1 was for elder people, 1 was for moms that wanted to walk on something soft when walking around the house, etc etc

It's quite easy to set this up even today if you're a beginner.

No need for super complex funnels at the start imo. Minimum time investment required.
