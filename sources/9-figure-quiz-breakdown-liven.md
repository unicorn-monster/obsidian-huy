Liven has over 4 million site visitors per month 😳 You and your entire family have probably been served one of their ads before. Their secret? Every single ad runs to a quiz funnel.

![[liven-01.png]]

Liven encourages self discovery through an app. They have mastered direct to consumer funnels. While their ads are strong direct response ads, their quiz funnels are a masterpiece.

![[liven-02.png]]

Framing the quiz as a "Personalized Well Being Plan." You're going down their funnel while believing you're receiving the value (a free plan). Starting with a very cognitively easy question.

![[liven-03.png]]

Right after gender then age, they show you the social proof — quick trust building rapport with 2.5 MILLION people.

![[liven-04.png]]

These are low cognitive questions they bombard you with. Easy to answer (yes, no, maybe style) so they can gather a ton of data. Also increasing your "sunken cost fallacy" making it unlikely to exit the funnel.

![[liven-05.png]]

Same for these... what I call doctor prescription. A doctor doesn't walk into the room and tell you what's wrong. He asks you questions, which lead you to believe something is wrong. "Do you often notice yourself getting light headed when standing up too fast..."

![[liven-06.png]]

They have also mastered changing up question format — multiple different formats, images, etc. Keeping viewers engaged, and preventing them from zoning out. Which is one of the main issues we see: boring, low cognitive funnels.

![[liven-07.png]]

Same thing here — multiple question formats. Images, yes or no, 'on a scale of...'. All of these together are incredibly powerful.

![[liven-08.png]]

Strong scientific social proof at the end.

![[liven-09.png]]

Your plan will be reviewed by our science team. The ultimate personalization to ensuring this is specifically for you. I mean, the science team must be accurate right?

![[liven-10.png]]

Then we end up with our personal profile — after hitting our email to be retargeted like a mfer. These personal profiles work super well for diagnosis based products.

![[liven-11.png]]

The loading screen forces users to stare at this screen for 5-10 seconds. Which creates belief in results. But also gives you a window to force whatever social proof, before and after photos you want, in front of them.

![[liven-12.png]]

Future pacing to make me hopeful in my results.

![[liven-13.png]]

Finally, a custom plan curated just for me (with daily pricing). Insane funnel. Brilliant conversion techniques. I will be cloning this for one of my offers soon.
