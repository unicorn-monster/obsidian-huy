Had the chance to look at data on Prism AI by @aliapopups

The popup reads who's on the site, and rebuilds the pop-up based on the customer's background.

That means a first-visit person who is a woman may see one version, a guy sees another, a returning customer sees something else.

It learns what converts for each type and keeps serving more of that.

The sofa brand running it (Sofa Club) pulled +21% email signups, +15% SMS, and +17% more attributed orders off the same popup.

We're seeing similar results for our brands as we've been experimenting with the feature.

I expect automated experiences that customize based on the user will be on every site soon.
