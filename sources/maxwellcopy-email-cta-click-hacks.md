4 easy hacks to get more clicks in your emails:

1) Keep the email short and snappy, under 10 words of copy so the button is super clear.
2) When you're running an offer, say "click here to activate" so the tap unlocks the discount.
3) Drop a "click for good luck" next to the button. People tap it just for fun.
4) Add a "magic button" for the curious people who can't help themselves.

Try them, this little stuff works stupidly well lol.
