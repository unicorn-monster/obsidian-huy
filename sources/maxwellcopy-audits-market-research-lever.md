title: Doing client audits weekly as best market research

Most founders at my size have completely stopped doing their own audits.

They've got teams for that now or someone under them handles it...

I still do every single one. 6 to 10 audits a week, every week.

And I don't think I'll ever fully give them up.

Every audit teaches me something... even the 7-figure brands show me stuff I can take straight back to my team.

It's the best market research there is. I see exactly what's working, where brands are stuck, and what they're actually struggling with, and that goes straight into the content and ads we run to get more brands.

Also... when a brand comes off my content and then see I'm the one who runs their audit, it hits completely different than handing them to someone they've never heard of.

One of the highest revenue generating ideas I can do.
