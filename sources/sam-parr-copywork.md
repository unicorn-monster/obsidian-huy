Interview Sam Parr tren podcast Sweat Equity "Become a Dangerously Good Writer in 55 Minutes". Doan 27:40-33:11: copywork. (Co quang cao Richpanel ~30:40-31:24, khong phai noi dung.)

## Copywork la gi
Tim doan writing minh thich (hoac duoc coi la hay noi chung) va CHEP TAY lai tung chu, word for word. Ngoi 1 tieng chep. Sam lam suot nhieu thang.

## Cach no thang short-form (case study host)
Video Instagram cu ket o 100-200k view. Host lam copywork: lay 5 script/video trieu view, chep lai, boc ra cac element (transition word, "but this happened, therefore that happened", cho cai tension). Doi chieu voi RETENTION GRAPH xem nguoi ta drop o dau (vd giay 12) -> chen attention/curiosity gap moi dung cho do. Video ke ra cong duoc 2 trieu view, chi mat 2 ngay.

## Analogy hoc nhac cu
Khong ai bao "ngoi vao piano viet hit song" ma lam duoc. Hoc bang cach copy: Jingle Bells -> Happy Birthday -> kho dan -> roi Foo Fighters/Green Day/Lady Gaga. Chep nhieu thi thay RULES va TEXTURE cach nguoi khac lam, roi moi "steal cai nay, steal cai kia, combine thanh cua minh". Nguyen tac: blindly copy cho den khi tim ra rules of the language minh dang muon noi.

## Ap cho moi thu, khong chi writing
Sam hoc humor bang chep SNL scripts; hoc beat bang recreate beat cua Kanye. "Bat cu thu gi muon co good taste, viec so 1 la copy mu cho toi khi ra luat."

## Chep TAY, khong go
Co science: nho tot hon khi viet vat ly (physical) so voi go phim. Phai cam duoc texture. Lien he therapy: viet tay giup transform.
