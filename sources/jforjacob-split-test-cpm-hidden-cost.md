title: Split test CVR lift hidden CPM cost on Meta
---

You split test your page

Intelligems says CVR and RPV up 10%

You roll out change

CVR and RPV don't actually change (or even get worse)

Why you may ask?

Because changes on your page effect Meta and other ad channels

CPM's can rise, targeting can shift etc

Unfortunately split testing tools don't give you insight on this

You could have a test showing in Intelligems that is bearing control by 20% but what you don't see is that your CPM's have risen by 40% for traffic going to that variant

So whenever you roll out a change you should monitor your in platform metrics for the next week or so otherwise that "winner" you thought you had could actually be a loser

Not enough people ever think about this
