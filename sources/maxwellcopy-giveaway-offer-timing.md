title: Giveaways only work as attention grab to offer
---

Please, for the love of God, stop running giveaways...

Most people run giveaways completely wrong, and it ends up hurting their results.

For example, if you run a month-long giveaway for a $2,000 chair and somebody opts in on the 5th of the month, are they buying anything for the next four weeks?

No. They're going to wait to see if they win, and of course they're not going to win. By the time that happens, they are a cold lead now. It's been too long.

Instead, just use giveaways as the attention grab...

Get them towards a special offer.

This email showcases it perfectly.

The only way to properly do giveaways.
