Running a subscription brand looks cute and sexy until you see what's going on inside…

- Unprofitable until the 3rd+ purchase
- Calling a 0.9x ROAS a win
- Profit constrained because of unprofitability at acquisition
- One bad month away from the whole thing blowing up
- Customer service a nightmare from upset rebilled customers
- Getting tempted to use grey hat and black hat strategies by the day
- Juggling multiple offer and funnel tests running at once
- Can't get clean retention cohort data for 6+ months
- Claude coding custom dashboards to track what you actually want
- Using 20+ different softwares at once
- Talent is tough to find with niche subscription experience
- Often stuck with budget agencies doing the bare minimum
- Copycat brands popping up everywhere

Most of these brands are LARPing success.

Very few are actually crushing it profitably. I know because I see it every time I audit one lol

But the ones that ARE winning happen to work with us
