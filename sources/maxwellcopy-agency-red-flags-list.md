Agency red flags:

- Never reports anything bad. There is always bad stuff going on. If they're only telling you good news they're either not paying attention or they're hiding something
- The person communicating with you isn't the person leading strategy on your account
- Agrees with everything you say
- Only looks at in-platform metrics like Klaviyo revenue instead of macro store goals and KPIs
- Delivers statistic reports but no actual insights or learnings from those reports
- Everything is an add-on. Always trying to upsell/cross-sell you
- Uses the same monthly templated report deck every single time
- Doesn't deliver a quick win within the first week
- Always trying to run discounts
- Locks you into 6-12 month contracts
- Makes big guarantees in the sales process
- Disorganized onboarding process
- Is vague when you ask about their internal processes
- No set weekly reporting cadence
- Doesn't respond within one hour on Slack
- No A/B testing
- Doesn't work in advance, always delivering work close to deadline
- Team members aren't full time, uses contractors
- Salesperson doesn't have domain-specific experience
- No audit delivered in the sales process
- Uses their own attribution settings
