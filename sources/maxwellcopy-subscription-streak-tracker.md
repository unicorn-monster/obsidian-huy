title: Subscription streak tracker tactic to cut churn

New subscription trend you should hop on...

Customers having too much product is what kills most subscriptions. People forget to take it, so it piles up, and they cancel...

So... build a custom tracker where customers log in every day and check off that they took the product.

If you miss a day, the streak resets...and if you hit 70 days in a row, a big reward is unlocked.

Now, will everyone actually log in daily? Probably not.

But it drills in the one habit that keeps people subscribed: using the product every day.

If I ran this, I'd add tiers so everyone has a reason to stay in it:

- 50% of days → small free gift in your next order
- 75% → a bigger offer
- 90% → an even bigger one
- 100% → An even bigger bigger one

Pair this with reminders, recovery, and celebrations emails and it becomes a retention engine.
