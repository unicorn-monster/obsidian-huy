We're seeing founder videos crush right now.

But not in ads like you'd expect...

Everyone's leaning hard into founder-led content/ads/emails on the front end, but almost NOBODY is using it anywhere else...

Put a founder video in:

- Your post-purchase upsell.
- In your onboarding thank-you
- On your new product launch hype pages
- Most importantly, on the splash page the second someone tries to cancel their subscription.

We added a founder video to one brand's cancel flow and TRIPLED their churn recovery rate just by putting a face to the name when someone went to cancel.

I expect this to become more and more popular as people catch on.
