Building and running WellTV across our accounts has made one thing really clear to me...

Most brands have no idea what their actual retention looks like.

They can see things like their revenue and their general repeat purchase rate and go no deeper.

But the numbers that ACTUALLY decide whether the brand will make it in the long run aren't tracked anywhere at all...

Now that it's live, the stuff we're pulling out is wild.

We can see the exact window someone's most likely to reorder, down to the product they bought.

We can tell you a Product A buyer is worth double a Product B buyer over 12 months, and know exactly when they are likely to buy.

We can see which email campaigns actually create repeat customers instead of just first-time revenue (something Klaviyo will never show you on its own).

We can see what funnels on ads produce the highest value customers LTV wise to report that back to the acquisition team.

Six months ago brands were guessing at all of this.

Now we are able to make educated decisions based on our numbers.

It's been a huge shift from just running a brand's email to actually being part of how their business grows.

Clients have been loving it too.
