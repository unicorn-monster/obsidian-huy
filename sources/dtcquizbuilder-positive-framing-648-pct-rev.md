648% rev increase in 3 months.

It just started ripping when we flipped the framing from negative to positive.

Instead of the typical, "start with the problem. Meet them where they're at."

We showed them where they wish they could be & how it's possible

Most of your traffic has already been burned.

They've tried the "miracle solutions."

Some even bought from brands selling the exact same mechanism as you.

So when they hit your quiz, they've quietly already decided it won't work.

Lead with the problem… and you just confirmed what they already feel.

So we flipped the first few questions.

Now they picture the outcome instead. Solving it.

Becoming their past self again.

Two things happened:

They stuck it out to the end — because now they want to see how they get there. Retention through the quiz jumped.

And by the time we positioned our mechanism (why ours beats the ones that failed them, why they should lock in more upfront)… they were already sold.

Higher completion. Higher AOV.

648% in 3 months. Just from changing where we start.
