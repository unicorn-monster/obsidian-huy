4 months in

Scaled past $500k / month

Things we've done:

- increased creative output

- onboarded new creators every week

- hired an ad creative agency & email agency

- read reviews, ad comments and customer service tickets manually to actually understand the customer

- made ads that drive real TOF (net new visitors)

- tested prices & offers (we actually increased prices AND shipping costs)

- negotiated better pricing with our manufacturer and 3PL due to increased volume

Things we haven't done:

- added new marketing channels

- discussed ad account structures

- built custom dashboards

- launched new products (too early)

- raised money or taken on debt
