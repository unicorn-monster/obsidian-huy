tl;dr - noticed my email list shrinking. Asked guy on my team: "Everything looks good." He was wrong.

Connected account to Fable. Within 5 minutes found that over 19,000 people had been auto-unsubscribed.

Reason why - automation someone set up years ago.

Yes, I know you should segment inactives. But also, I know I had deliverability issues a while back.

Now Fable is scrubbing the roughly 19k people and setting up the reengagement campaigns. Doing it carefully and in tranches. Being put into a separate list, not my main one obviously.

Value to Me:
Well when I was emailing daily, average subscriber on my list was "worth" $1k per year. Math on that is simple. 10k subscribers and list made me at least $1 mill per year. Realistically more like $2 mill but we'll keep it conservative.

So let's assume just 3k of the 19k get reactivated. If I'm a good boy and email actively, worth an extra $300k per year minimum.

Pays for that Max Plan and some Fable Credits I'd say.

Conclusion:
AI is amazing and people who think AI isn't valuable have zero clue what they are talking about.
