title: Two native ad iterations from winning long-form

For everyone running long form native ads, try these iterations. They perform really well:

1. Grab your winning long form and turn it into a scrolling text video with voiceover. Use the winning image as the background.

2. Turn it into an AI animation story. This takes some effort to do well, but it scales.
