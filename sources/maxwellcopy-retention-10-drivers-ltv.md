Do you want to increase your 6-month LTV by 50%???

That doesn't happen from AB testing…

What actually drives mass improvements in retention:

> New product drops, new flavors, new variants. People buy again and stay subscribed when there's something to look forward to.

> New and creative offers… not basic percent off deals

> Proper product onboarding and education so people use it right and see the results (extremely key)

> A product or message tied to the customer's identity. When the brand becomes part of who they are, they keep buying (think AG1 style)

> Subscriber communication DIALED to be optimized on reducing cancellations… focus on billing reminders, order confirmations, etc. All the unsexy stuff most brands ignore.

> Offers that actually align to the customers consumption patterns (Don't hit someone on a 30-day rebill with a 90-serving offer).

> Replenishment reminders timed properly based on your data. Timing > messaging for replenishment.

> Free gifts and incentives with each additional purchase that ACTUALLY are good.

> An Apple-level unboxing experience.

> A clean, active email and SMS list.

These are what actually matter for retention.
