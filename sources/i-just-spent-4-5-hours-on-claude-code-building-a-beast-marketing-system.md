I just spent 4.5 hours playing with Claude Code to build landing pages... here's my EXACT strategy. It starts in the ad account.

![[cc-marketing-01.jpeg]]

## The FB Setup
With anon Facebook profiles: "The Tooth Blog," "Foot Expert," "Cardio Diary." Something along these lines — if you ran an authority info site in a specific niche. The goal of this is to build something organic looking. It gets much lower CPMs and you can also run some riskier ads. The goal is cheap clicks, calling out specific problems.

![[cc-marketing-02.jpeg]]

## The Domain
The goal is to keep everything organic. Get a domain that matches this — you don't want to run it on your normal domain.

![[cc-marketing-03.jpeg]]

(Yes, 3rd party tracking is important here.) Make sure to track: sessions, bounce rate, CTR — ideally as much post-back tracking to each page as possible. Example: oral hygiene brand. Domain: dentistdiary.com. FB page: "The Dentistry Diary."

## Cracking Claude Code
First off, find 3 advertorials / listicles or page templates you like — maybe even ones you're running. There's a ton of inspo on crazy organic advertorials that RIP all over X. Simply ask Claude to build a **skill** based on these landing pages + your brand data. The goal is to just get the page outline — the template. This will make your life SO easy down the line. Imagine a Funnelish/Shopify template... in Claude, that simply just needs updated copy and images. Once it's got it accurate, you ask it to match it with your brand design (legit just plug your URL in, it will pull the data).

In the skill, add 3 pages you like — can add more later, or just start off with one for the first build. Now every time you create a new chat, you can pull these page templates in. Just update the copy and boom, it's ready to go.

## Connecting the Domain
This was super cool. Within my Claude Code terminal, I asked it how to connect to Vercel. It gave me some prompts to plug into my terminal. I followed it step by step — within 5 mins my Vercel was connected. Now, every page I create... I can just spin it live within a second under this domain: dentistdiary.com/root-canal, dentistdiary.com/rotten-teeth, dentistdiary.com/best-toothpaste.

![[cc-marketing-04.png]]

## The Final Piece — Image Gen
I searched up "Higgsfield API," knowing it's almost impossible they don't have an API. Of course they do. I plugged the documentation into Claude, page by page — press "Copy Page" in the top right. Then I created an API key. It took about 5 minutes, literally. Higgsfield was connected.

I'm trying to find a simple workflow to make the image quality better here — but I did just drag in a top performing ad for a new angle & in one try it spat out a landing page for me to use off the RIP. Told it to connect it to the domain, and it did.
