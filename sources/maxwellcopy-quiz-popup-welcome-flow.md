So many brands run quiz pop-ups but do NOTHING with it...

Meanwhile if you customize your welcome flow around the answers, you can get an easy increase in conversions.

One of the easiest lifts you can get:

- Run a quiz pop-up (@AliaPopups makes it easy and high converting)
- Tag people by their answer
- Customize the first 1-2 emails in the welcome flow to match what they chose

And that's it.

Super simple and easy.
