just finished scaling a supps brand from $155k to $242k in monthly google ads rev

took us 30 days and a full advertorial build-out

no meta, no tiktok, no AI UGC bs

so i decided to put together a full breakdown of the ENTIRE system...

**The advertorial architecture:**

3 pages built per ICP angle, each a hybrid between an editorial and a sales page matched to the exact search intent hitting it, walking the reader from the pain they searched into the mechanism and then the product, so cold traffic that was bouncing off the PDP started converting profitably within 2 weeks.

**The YouTube Shorts MOF stack:**

8 raw, UGC-style creatives mirroring the advertorial angles launched in week 1, 2 winners pulling 60%+ view-through rates, building remarketing depth inside the same platform the search traffic came from.

**The Shopping feed rebuild:**

Product titles restructured so the first 3 words are the highest-converting non-branded keywords pulled from the search terms report. Custom labels tiering SKUs by margin so Smart Bidding pushes the highest-margin products. Efficiency gains that freed budget for the upper funnel without inflating CPA.

**The PMax restructure:**

The generic one-asset-group build killed off and rebuilt into one ICP per campaign, each with its own creatives, headlines, and advertorial landing page, fed customer match lists and custom intent signals from the converting search terms report, hitting branded-search CPAs on cold acquisition.

**The full audience and signals infrastructure:**

Customer match segments as the signal anchor Google reverse-engineers for lookalikes. RLSA layers bucketed by funnel stage so checkout abandoners get bid hardest. YouTube view-through audiences closing the loop between Shorts and the conversion layer. Micro-conversion optimisation on add-to-cart while Smart Bidding learns.

All backed by £10M+ in client revenue managed through Google Ads full-funnel ecosystems.
