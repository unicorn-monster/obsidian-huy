11 UNSPOKEN RULES OF 9-FIGURE SUBSCRIPTION BRANDS:

1) Don't send more than 3 emails per month to your active subscribers.

2) Aggressively test and customize your billing reminders. Transactional looking emails push people to cancel.

3) Never look at Klaviyo's placed order revenue. It includes subscription rebills. Create a custom metric that excludes them.

4) Match subscription cadence to how users use the product. Don't try to sell too much up front. Too much product is the #1 reason people cancel.

5) Subscription-maxx on your PDP pages. Auto-select subscription, add a 30+90 day sub option, aggressively discount.

6) Getting a subscriber means nothing if you don't onboard them right. Push the habit, educate, and build community.

7) Never promise fast results to close the sale. Sell the 90+ day commitment before you see results instead.

8) Don't allow subscribers to pause their subscription only delay. Nobody ever unpauses their subscription and it messes up your metrics. Push them to delay or just cancel.

9) Customize incentives in your cancel flows. Offer skips or delays for people who have too much product. Discounts for people worried about price.

10) Don't measure wins by take rate alone. Take rate means nothing if they all cancel by Order 3.

Fix these and you'll stop bleeding subscribers every month.
