i've fixed 100+ google ads funnels....

here are the 7 mistakes i see in almost every single one:

1. trusting a blended ROAS number. if 80% of your conversions come from branded search, your "7x ROAS" is mostly people who would've bought anyway. pull it apart by campaign type first.

2. treating PMax as a strategy. one campaign, all products, generic assets, no audience signals. the algorithm finds the path of least resistance, which is always your existing branded demand. then it takes credit for it.

3. confusing budget increases with scale. you can't scale a demand-capture-only account by spending more. you hit the ceiling of how much intent exists for your brand and category. more budget just makes that ceiling more expensive.

4. sending all traffic to the product page. cold traffic needs education before it buys. an advertorial between the ad and the PDP consistently doubles cold CVR in almost every account i test it on.

5. ignoring the shopping feed. the product title is the primary matching signal for shopping queries. if it starts with your brand name, you're invisible to anyone who doesn't already know you.

6. zero negative keyword hygiene. i've opened accounts running for 18 months with no negatives. hundreds of pounds a month going to queries that could never convert.

7. switching to target ROAS before the campaign has enough data. the algorithm needs 30-50 conversions to learn properly. switch before that and it never stabilises.

every single one of these is fixable. none of them require a bigger budget.
