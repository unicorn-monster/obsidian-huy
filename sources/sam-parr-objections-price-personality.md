## Xu objection bang personal anecdote (manh hon noi thang "san pham tot")

## Building desire around price (Harley case)
Ad Harley gio toan "tiet kiem bao nhieu neu pre-qualify" (bottom-of-funnel, khong sai voi nguoi high-intent), nhung cach hay hon: build desire truoc, gia chi la bonus. Sam goi y: "Nguoi mua trai nghiem hanh phuc hon X lan nguoi mua do vat. May thay cai nay la ca hai... va con tiet kiem chut tien. Two for one." Frame mua motor = mua EXPERIENCE (experiences make you happier), gia dat phia sau nhu phan thuong.
