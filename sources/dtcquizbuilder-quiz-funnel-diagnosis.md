Here's how we read quiz funnels

To take funnels that perform like shit

To becoming #1 performers in the ad account

(Exact process)

---

1. Very poor start rate

In the first image you see a 17% start rate (people starting the quiz)

2nd image has a 31% start rate

Here you're looking at ads & positioning on the Q1

> What is the ad saying
> What is the first question saying

Make sure the offer matches the alignment of where the ad left off & makes it promising enough for them to continue the quiz

---

2. Quiz to purchase rate (how many people complete the quiz then buy)

This is based on how much intent the quiz builds.

Is it really selling them? Or is it operating as a barricade to the offer?

If it's building intent, you're going to sit between a 15% > 25% completion to purchase ratio

If it's just an additional step > you'll be under 15%

Here you need to truly ask yourself, have I really made the customer feel like they have a problem? And given them a logical reason why they need to purchase today?

---

Positioning

The positioning of a quiz determines how well it performs here

One lacks positioning, customers do not understand why they're going through the quiz

Outcome is unclear

The other has a strong position. It matches the underlying message in the ad account.

Customers know what the outcome truly is

almost 3x more revenue from 1 simple fix
