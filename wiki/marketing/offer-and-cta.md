# Offer + CTA mechanics

Beat 14-15 của [[ugly-ads-method]]. Nguồn: [[maxpro-skeleton-and-banks]], [[kittysupps-swipe-lines]], [[kittysupps-brand-offer]].

## CTA framework (transferable)
- **Rescue + warning**, không "buy now".
- **Deny the sale:** "I'm not affiliated with them", "I forgot to add the link." Denying the sale IS the sale.
- **Inoculation lock:** "don't buy on Amazon, they're counterfeit" -> lock click về nguồn + pre-frame review xấu là "người mua hàng fake". Xuất hiện ở MỌI script taurine.
- **Risk reversal cụ thể:** "$39.95, 99-day money-back, used OK, họ trả ship về. 1 lần skip groomer là hoàn vốn. Không có kịch bản nào bạn lỗ."
- **The cause (ownable):** "donate 50% cho no-kill shelter, $40k+, 1,000 chó cứu."
- Link lặp 2-3x (body + EDIT + P.S.).

## Footer link-caption bank (mỗi cái = mini-hook, xem [[hooks]])
"She Hasn't Let Me Touch Her Paws in 8 Years. Until This." · "My 11-Yr-Old Ran Again. It Was Never Her Age." · "Groomers Don't Want You Owning This ($45 a Visit)" · "I Sedated My Dog 14 Times For Nothing😢" · footer brand: "MAXIMUTT.COM | 18,587+ Calmer Dogs | 99-Day Guarantee | 50% to Shelters".

## Offer + price anchoring
- MaxPro: $39.95 (từ $66.60, -40%), 99-day, company-paid return, no free-gift.
- KittySupps price-anchor playbook: anchor vào pain cost ($2,300 surgery vs $39 jar) + đối thủ ($22 Amazon "fake" vs giá mình). Curiosity-gap pricing: "just go look at the price, I'm serious" (giấu giá ép click).
